Samosa

Ingredient

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon crushed ginger
  • 1 teaspoon raisins
  • 5 boiled potato
  • 5 boiled potato
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon kasoori methi leaves
  • 1 teaspoon carom seeds
  • 1/4 cup water

Recipe

  1. Step 1: Sauté cumin seeds for potato filling
  2. Step 2: Add spices and boiled potatoes and cook for a while
  3. Step 3: Prepare the dough for the Samosa.
  4. Step 4: Roll the dough in small puris and cut into half.
  5. Step 5: Fill the semi-circle with potato filling and deep fry.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredient

  • 1/2 tin Nestlé MILKMAID
  • 200 gms Sooji
  • 600 gms Paneer
  • 200 gms Maida
  • 5 boiled potato
  • 3-4 Green Cardamom
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 kg Sugar
  • 2L Water

Recipe

  1. Step 1: Mix crumbled paneer, maida, sooji, baking soda, baking powder, and Nestlé MILKMAID, and knead to make a smooth dough. Roll the mixture into small round gulab jamuns.
  2. Step 2: Add water and sugar in a pan on medium flame to prepare sugar syrup. Once the sugar dissolves, cook it for another 10 minutes on low flame and add crushed cardamom.
  3. Step 3: Now fry the gulab jamun balls in oil at low medium flame. Once they turn brown, transfer them immediately to the sugar syrup. Keep them aside for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Whip the cream and fill in a piping bag. Microwave the grated milk chocolate for 1 minute and keep aside.
  5. Step 5: For plating, cut the prepared gulab jamuns into pieces and place them in a glass. Make alternative layers of whipped cream, melted chocolate and gulab jamun pieces. Garnish it with some nuts and enjoy!
